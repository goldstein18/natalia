OUR MANUFACTURING PROCESS





  • DESIGN

    _______________





    Our Transformers are designed using a modern computerized system to achieve the optimal balance between cost and efficiency.

  • CORE

    _______________



    We use rectangular "Wescore" type cores constructed from grain-oriented silicon steel sheet, which ensures the transformer will operate quietly and with low losses.

  • COILS

    _______________



    Also known as windings. Copper enameled wire is used in high voltage, and aluminum strip in low voltage. These are insulated with insulating paper that stabilizes insulation against dehydration, hydrolysis, and oxidation, enabling higher operating temperatures without sacrificing the Transformer's lifespan.

  • TANK

    _______________



    It is made of carbon steel and/or stainless steel with micro-wire welding. Its design is determined by calculating the mechanical stresses (pressure, vacuum, short-circuit, and transportation) to which it will be subjected.

  • Insulating Liquid

    _______________

    The oil used complies with the IEEE C57.12.00 standard and undergoes dielectric strength tests. Eco-friendly vegetable-based FR3 dielectric oil with a low flammability point is also used.

POWDER PAINT SYSTEM

_______________________________________________



The powder paint is deposited by an electrostatic method, a system of charges regulated by positive and negative ions and subsequently cured in a drying oven at 250ºC. This guarantees the high quality requirements in terms of adhesion and salt chambers according to ASTM standards.


We have the most convenient powder painting system from Nordson (ENCORE POWDER SPRAY SYSTEM) so we guarantee 100% quality, improving efficiency, flexibility and productivity in our painting process. These are some of the great advantages:


POWDER PAINT SYSTEM

_______________________________________________



1. Thicknesses between 5 and 7 thousandths, guaranteeing durability.
2. Uniformity in thickness across the entire surface of the tank.
3. Unbeatable adhesion.
4. Compliance with IEEE specification (anti-corrosion coatings).
5. Greater durability and corrosion resistance.

POWDER PAINT SYSTEM

_______________________________________________



The tank made of carbon steel sheet and/or stainless steel with microwire welding, is subjected to the following preparation processes to be painted:

1. Sandblasting (erosive attack with 25 grain steel shot and 200 pounds of pressure).
2. Chemical degreaser (ST-30 at a temperature of 70 ºC).
3. Rinse.
4. Cleaning (attack with KO-50 iron phosphate).
5. Rinse.
6. Organic sealing (SL-06 temperature of 80 ºC).
